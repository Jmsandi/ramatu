Our Approach

At the Ramatu Foundation, we take a holistic approach to tackling challenges faced by girls and women in Sierra Leone. Our programs focus on education, awareness, mentorship, and community engagement to create sustainable change.

Each of our initiatives is carefully designed to address specific needs within our communities while building towards our broader mission of liberation and empowerment for all girls and women.

Our Core Programs

Educational Support

We provide educational materials, scholarships, and school fees for vulnerable girls, helping them stay in school and build a brighter future.

  • School supplies distribution
  • Scholarship programs
  • Tuition assistance

Our Vision

To see a safe Sierra Leone wherein all the girls and women are aware of their rights and responsibilities and are able to make their dreams come to reality.

Established 2019 in Sierra Leone

"Making it happen for sustainable change"

Our Mission

To be an effective agent in the liberation of the oppressed and marginalized girls and women from all forms of violence that stop them from achieving their economic, social, emotional and physical potential.
