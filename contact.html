Frequently Asked Questions

What are your office hours?

Our office in Makeni is open Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. However, we recommend making an appointment before visiting to ensure staff availability.

How can I donate to the foundation?

There are several ways to donate. You can make a bank transfer, use mobile money in Sierra Leone, or visit our "Get Involved" page for more information. We appreciate all contributions, no matter the size.

How can I volunteer with the foundation?

We welcome volunteers both in Sierra Leone and internationally. Visit our "Get Involved" page for information on volunteer opportunities or email us directly at sramatukarim@gmail.com.

Do you provide receipts for donations?

Yes, we provide receipts for all donations. Please ensure you include your contact information with your donation so we can send you a receipt.

How can organizations partner with you?

We welcome partnerships with organizations that share our vision. Please email us at sramatukarim@gmail.com with details about your organization and potential areas of collaboration.

Our Vision

To see a safe Sierra Leone wherein all the girls and women are aware of their rights and responsibilities and are able to make their dreams come to reality.

Established 2019 in Sierra Leone

"Making it happen for sustainable change"

Our Mission

To be an effective agent in the liberation of the oppressed and marginalized girls and women from all forms of violence that stop them from achieving their economic, social, emotional and physical potential.
