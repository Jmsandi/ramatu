Our Vision

To see a safe Sierra Leone wherein all the girls and women are aware of their rights and responsibilities and are able to make their dreams come to reality.

Established 2019 in Sierra Leone

"Making it happen for sustainable change"

Our Mission

To be an effective agent in the liberation of the oppressed and marginalized girls and women from all forms of violence that stop them from achieving their economic, social, emotional and physical potential.
