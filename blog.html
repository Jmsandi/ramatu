Featured Program

Educational Outreach Initiatives

Our educational outreach team visits schools in Bombali district, Makeni through our 'school tours initiative' to educate school pupils during assemblies about perennial issues affecting girls such as early & forced marriage, teenage pregnancy, rape, good touch & bad touch, menstrual hygiene, infectious diseases like typhoid & malaria.

We inspire girls to stay in school and emphasize the importance of girls' education, while addressing gender-based violence in schools and encouraging boys to be allies for gender equality.

This initiative is part of our broader effort to reduce school dropout rates among girls, place a zero tolerance on girls' issues, reduce gender-based violence amongst school pupils, and promote gender equality in the education sector.

Healthcare Initiatives

Community Health, Wellbeing and Emergency Response

We provide sexual reproductive health & rights education to our community girls and women; childbirth control, body autonomy, use of contraceptives, abstinence for girls, sexually transmitted diseases, menstrual hygiene management & sanitary products distribution, and understanding the female reproductive organ.

By empowering women and girls with knowledge about their bodies and health rights, we're helping them make informed decisions for themselves and their families thus enabling safe, healthy, informed and happy resilient communities.
