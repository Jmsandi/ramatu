What We Do

Since 2019, we've been working in Bombali district and visited schools in Port Loko district, directly working with 4 communities in Makeni to create sustainable change.

Education & Rights

Educating women & girls about their rights & responsibilities and educating men and boys to be Allies for Gender Equality. Providing financial and material support for 40+ girls to achieve high levels of education.

Anti-Violence Campaigns

Campaigning against gender-based violence including forced marriage, Female Genital Mutilation (FGM), rape, sexual harassment, teenage pregnancy and early marriage.

Health & Wellbeing

Promoting Sexual Reproductive Health & Rights - providing sex education and contraception. Responding to health emergencies like COVID-19, Typhoid & Malaria with awareness and resources.

Community Engagement

Educating local communities through community days, broadcast and social media, and school visits (school tours). Organizing motivational/mentorship programs for girls and women in the communities we work with.

Girls Linking Program

Linking girls in the Foundation with those in UK schools to share cultures and inspire each other. We work with women in the UK through Zoom meetings to share stories and inspire our teams.
