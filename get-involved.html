Volunteer Opportunities

We welcome volunteers who share our vision and want to contribute their time, skills, and energy to our cause. There are many ways to get involved, both in Sierra Leone and internationally.

Roles for Local Volunteers (Sierra Leone)

Community Outreach

Help us engage with communities, conduct awareness sessions, and organize events.

Mentorship

Serve as a mentor to girls in our educational support program, providing guidance and inspiration.

Program Support

Assist with the implementation of our various programs, from educational support to rights awareness.

Administration

Help with office administration, record-keeping, and logistics to keep our operations running smoothly.

Roles for International Volunteers

Virtual Mentorship

Connect with girls in Sierra Leone through our virtual mentorship program, sharing knowledge and experiences.

Fundraising

Organize fundraising events or campaigns in your community to support our work.

Skills-Based Volunteering

Contribute your professional skills in areas such as marketing, website development, grant writing, and more.

Advocacy

Raise awareness about girls' rights and gender equality issues in Sierra Leone in your community and networks.

How to Apply

If you're interested in volunteering with the Ramatu Foundation, you can apply using our online form or send an email to sramatukarim@gmail.com with the following information:

Apply Online Now
  • Your name and contact information
  • Your location (country/city)
  • The type of volunteer role you're interested in
  • Your relevant skills and experience
  • Your availability (hours per week and duration)
  • A brief statement about why you want to volunteer with the Ramatu Foundation

We'll get back to you within a week to discuss potential volunteer opportunities and next steps.

Partner With Us

Partnerships are essential to expanding our impact and creating sustainable change. We welcome collaborations with organizations, businesses, schools, and institutions that share our vision.

Types of Partnerships

Program Partnerships

Collaborate with us on implementing specific programs or projects that align with our mission, such as educational initiatives, skills training, or awareness campaigns.

Corporate Sponsorships

Businesses can sponsor specific programs, events, or initiatives, providing financial support while demonstrating corporate social responsibility.

Technical Support

Organizations with specific expertise can provide technical support in areas such as healthcare, education, legal services, or technology to enhance our programs.

School/University Partnerships

Educational institutions can partner with us through exchange programs, research collaboration, or service-learning opportunities for students.

Benefits of Partnership

Social Impact

Contribute to meaningful change in the lives of girls and women in Sierra Leone.

Visibility

Recognition on our website, social media, and relevant promotional materials.

Community Engagement

Opportunities to engage with local communities in Sierra Leone.

Network Building

Connect with other organizations and stakeholders working on gender equality and development.

Contact Us for Partnership

If your organization is interested in partnering with the Ramatu Foundation, please email us at sramatukarim@gmail.com with:

  • Your organization's name and brief background
  • Contact person's information
  • Type of partnership you're interested in
  • Potential areas of collaboration
  • Proposed timeframe for partnership

Our Vision

To see a safe Sierra Leone wherein all the girls and women are aware of their rights and responsibilities and are able to make their dreams come to reality.

Established 2019 in Sierra Leone

"Making it happen for sustainable change"

Our Mission

To be an effective agent in the liberation of the oppressed and marginalized girls and women from all forms of violence that stop them from achieving their economic, social, emotional and physical potential.
